Awards / 2014

2014: the central year of the award archive.

The 2014 record spans international salon success, Hong Kong environmental advocacy, conservation, civic culture, heritage, and humanitarian photography.

International

Global recognition across storytelling, landscape, sport, and fine art.

The migrated research identifies 2014 as the central competitive year: National Geographic, Egypt, India, Jodhpur, and PX3 all sit in this phase. The breadth matters because the same eye moves across documentary, motion, civic space, and constructed impact.
