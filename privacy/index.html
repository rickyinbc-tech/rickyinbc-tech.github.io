Privacy notice

How inquiry and website data is handled.

This notice applies to rickykwok.com and to information sent through its contact and image-licensing forms.

Effective July 10, 2026

In brief

The studio collects only the information needed to understand, respond to, and document a request.

When you submit a form, the studio receives the information you enter, such as your name, email, organization, artwork of interest, intended use, deadline, budget range, and message. The form also records a lead ID, the page where the request was sent, the initial landing page, and available referrer information so the inquiry can be understood and attributed.

Do not submit payment-card details, identity documents, medical information, passwords, confidential source material, or other sensitive personal information. This website does not provide account registration or an online payment checkout.

Use and disclosure

Why the information is used.

Inquiry information is used for direct studio communication and related business records, not to create an unrelated marketing profile.

Responding

Information is used to assess and reply to print, exhibition, curatorial, licensing, press, and general inquiries and to provide requested follow-up.

Administration

Lead IDs and source details help prevent duplicate handling, connect follow-up to the correct work or project, and understand which pages lead to qualified requests.

Rights records

Where a request becomes an acquisition, exhibition, publication, or license, relevant correspondence may be kept as part of the transaction, permission, provenance, or rights record.

No mailing-list assumption

Submitting an inquiry does not subscribe you to a newsletter or unrelated promotional email. Any future subscription would require a separate choice.

Service providers

Forms, spam protection, and privacy.

The website relies on limited third-party services to deliver forms and reduce spam.

FormSubmit

Form submissions are processed by FormSubmit so they can be delivered to studio@rickykwok.com and an automatic confirmation can be sent to you. FormSubmit states that its submission archive is retained for 30 days. Its documentation and privacy terms apply to that processing.

reCAPTCHA

FormSubmit may present Google reCAPTCHA to distinguish people from automated abuse. Google’s privacy policy and terms apply when that service is used.

Visitor analytics disabled

The site does not currently use Google Analytics or another visitor-analytics service. It does not load the Google Analytics script or set first-party _ga analytics cookies.

Form data is not analytics data

Form names, email addresses, message text, lead IDs, and other entered form contents are used only to process the requested inquiry and are not transmitted to an analytics service.

Browser session storage

The site uses browser session storage to remember a query-sanitized landing page and referrer and, after a form attempt, the lead ID, inquiry type, artwork context, source page, and submission time. It does not store the entered name, email, organization, or message there. Session storage is limited to the current browser-tab session.

Necessary disclosure

Information may also be disclosed where reasonably necessary to obtain professional advice, protect rights or security, comply with a legal obligation, or complete a transaction you requested.

Retention and safeguards

Records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for their purpose.

FormSubmit states that its submission archive is retained for 30 days. Email copies and studio records may be retained separately while an inquiry is active or reasonably needed. Records connected with a sale, exhibition, publication, license, dispute, tax requirement, or other legal obligation may be kept longer.

Because visitor analytics is disabled, the site does not create a Google Analytics visitor-retention record.

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect studio records. No internet transmission or email system can be guaranteed completely secure, so please limit submissions to information necessary for the request.

You may ask to access, correct, or delete personal information held by the studio, subject to records that must reasonably be retained for legal, rights-management, security, or transaction purposes.
