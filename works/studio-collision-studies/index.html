Studio Collision Studies

Water, ash, flower, egg, powder, chalk, and flame photographed as controlled high-speed events.

Cluster Essay

Studio Collision Studies brings together the constructed side of Kwok’s practice.

Studio Collision Studies brings together the constructed side of Kwok’s practice. These works use controlled light, saturated colour, and high-speed timing to hold events that would normally vanish too quickly to be studied.

The cluster includes water forms, ash, flower, egg, powder, and liquid colour. It shows how the Collision series turns force into line, volume, atmosphere, and temporary sculpture.
