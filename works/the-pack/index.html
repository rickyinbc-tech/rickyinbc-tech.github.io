Artwork Details

The Pack

  • Series: Motion
  • Location: Horse racing track
  • Medium: Archival pigment print
  • Print: Edition and size details available on request.

The Pack is a study of speed under compression. Horses and jockeys crowd the frame as they lean through a turn, each body pushing against the next. Dirt lifts from the track, colours flash across silks, and the rail curves into the image as a hard boundary. Unlike the swimming works, this photograph does not dissolve the subject into blur; it concentrates pressure. The viewer feels the risk of proximity, the weight of bodies, and the decision-making inside a race. Kwok’s Motion series is not only about smooth movement. It also studies tension, density, and the moment when many forces compete for space. The Pack turns sport into a charged visual knot, where rhythm is built from hooves, bodies, colour, and dust.
