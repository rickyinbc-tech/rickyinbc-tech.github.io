Artwork Details

Dragon Night Festival

  • Series: Ritual
  • Location: Hong Kong
  • Status: Acquisition, exhibition, and licensing status is confirmed by the studio.

Dragon Night Festival carries ritual into the density of Hong Kong streets. The fire dragon moves through a crowded night scene, surrounded by smoke, warm light, spectators, and bodies in motion. Kwok’s composition gives the procession scale without losing the sensory pressure of being inside it. The image belongs equally to Ritual and City Light: it is a public ceremony, but it is also a study of urban light and collective movement. The dragon’s form appears both physical and atmospheric, half object and half trail of energy. The crowd is not background; it is part of the work’s force. The photograph holds a city moment when tradition, spectacle, smoke, and street architecture converge into one charged visual event.
