Project Context

Labour within a wetland landscape.

The photograph shows workers in waterproof clothing harvesting fish, with water and working equipment forming the immediate setting.

The Hong Kong Bird Watching Society framed its photography competition around fishponds as both landscape and wetland habitat. Its published result placed 漁獲 first in the student landscape category.

No first-person field account is added here because the institutional record does not publish one. The wider project page separates what can be observed in the photograph from what the official record verifies.
