Artwork Details

Street Procession

  • Series: City Light
  • Location: Hong Kong
  • Photographer: Ricky Kwok / 郭文棣 / Kwok Man Tai
  • Artwork type: Photograph
  • Copyright: © Ricky Kwok. Written permission is required for reproduction.

Street Procession is a quiet visual joke with serious compositional intelligence. Real pedestrians move along a wall where painted figures appear to march in the same direction. The photograph turns the sidewalk into a layered procession of bodies and images, public life and representation. Kwok’s timing is essential: the real people align with the mural closely enough that the boundary between street and artwork becomes unstable. The monochrome treatment strengthens the illusion and removes distraction. In the City Light and Hong Kong Urban Light contexts, this work shows that the city’s theatre is not only neon or festival; it can also appear in ordinary walking. The image is about participation in a shared visual field, where everyone on the street becomes part of a larger composition.
