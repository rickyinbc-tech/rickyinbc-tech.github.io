Artwork Details

Night Fishermen

  • Series: Ritual
  • Location: Li River region
  • Artist: Ricky Kwok / 郭文棣 / Kwok Man Tai
  • Artwork type: Photograph
  • Copyright: © Ricky Kwok. Written permission is required for reproduction.

Night Fishermen behaves visually like theatre: two figures carry glowing lamps while the mountains recede into blue-black darkness. Fish, baskets, raft, lamps, and silhouettes hold the picture between labour and myth. The public record currently supplies the visual caption only; exact date, location, working activity, subject relationship, direction, consent, and staging circumstances require the relevant field record before documentary or editorial use.
