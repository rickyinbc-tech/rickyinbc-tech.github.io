Profile

I study the moment when ordinary reality becomes unstable.

Ricky Kwok (Kwok Man Tai / 郭文棣) is a Hong Kong fine-art and documentary photographer. His practice includes Ritual, Collision, Motion, and City Light, alongside documented projects on light pollution and fishpond conservation.

  • Base: Hong Kong
  • Distinction: Associate of the Royal Photographic Society (ARPS)
  • Practice: Fine-art and documentary photography
  • Website: rickykwok.com

Selected recognition

Commended, Youth, Culture, 2015 Sony World Photography Awards; Champion, University of Hong Kong Light Pollution Photography Competition 2014, for Light Encroached Homes / 光染民居; four student-category placements in the Hong Kong Bird Watching Society’s 2014 fishpond competition; and Outstanding Award in the Secondary School Section of the 2013 「城市印象＠你我社區」Photography Competition.

Documented projects

Hong Kong light pollution and Hong Kong fishpond conservation, with institutional source links and artwork records.
