Awards & Recognition

A documented record of recognition across art, advocacy, motion, and Hong Kong light.

Ricky Kwok’s archive records more than 100 awards across Hong Kong and international photography circuits. This page keeps the record factual, source-backed, and connected to the works without turning the portfolio into a trophy list.

Catalog Scope

Every documented award found in the current research archive.

The catalog below merges the full award tables and narrative research published at blog.rickykwok.com with the existing public proof links on this site. Where legacy sources describe a broader 2013-2015 salon period rather than a precise date, the record is grouped by source context instead of forcing unsupported certainty. Exhibition or award claims without a clear public source are excluded from the confirmed list.
