Evidence Standard

Public wording follows the source.

Every published entry preserves the result, category, year, title, and name form found in the cited record. ARPS is a professional distinction, not a competition win; no unaudited “100+ awards” total is used.

Professional distinction & selected recognition

Six high-value public records.

The complete chronology below carries the wider record. These six entries are a concise introduction for curators, editors, and fact-checkers.

  • ARPSAssociate of the Royal Photographic Society
    An RPS distinction used after the artist’s name; it is kept separate from the competition record. Supporting record available for qualified fact-checking requests.
  • 2015Commended, Youth, Culture
    2015 Sony World Photography Awards. World Photography Organisation record
  • 2014Secondary-school Category Champion
    National Geographic Channel’s Nat Geo Awards Photography & Documentary Competition 2014, for 整整有條. Contemporaneous result report
  • 2014Champion, Light Pollution Photography Competition
    University of Hong Kong, for Light Encroached Homes / 光染民居; selected from 154 entries. HKU record
  • 2014Hong Kong Fishpond Conservation photography competition
    Student landscape champion for 漁獲; student landscape runner-up for 日出大生圍; student ecology champion for 閒來休息; student ecology third place for 美食當前. Hong Kong Bird Watching Society record
  • 2013First Place, School Group—Ships and Boats
    Harbour Business Forum’s Our Harbour Photo Competition. Organizer press-release PDF
  • 2016Merit Prize, Equal Opportunities Photo Competition
    Co-organized by the Equal Opportunities Commission and ten local tertiary institutions. HKMU record
