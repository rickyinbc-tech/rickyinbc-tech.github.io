Project Notes

Travel: China Documentary Studies expands the official archive with a focused body of work.

This project brings together documentary photographs from China and surrounding travel work: river scenes, kitchens, fields, craftsmen, fishpond labour, and village encounters.

The sequence is preserved as a room of its own so the rhythm between images remains legible, while the Selected Works page remains a tight exhibition edit.
