Photography Project / Nepal

Nepal Documentary

Portraits and daily scenes from Nepal, built around direct presence, family, work, and street life.

View PhotographsAll Projects

Project Notes

Eleven portraits and street scenes hold attention on people, gestures, and everyday surroundings.

Children, families, workers, and elders appear across kitchens, steps, yards, markets, roads, and street work. The photographs move between direct portraits and small exchanges of touch, labour, and care.

Eye contact, weathered surfaces, red cloth, window light, and open space give the sequence its visual continuity. Each scene remains distinct while the group builds a wider view of daily public life.

  • Project: Nepal Documentary
  • Focus: Documentary portraiture
  • Photographs: 11
  • First published: June 6, 2018 on Behance
