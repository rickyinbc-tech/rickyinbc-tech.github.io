Infrared Photography Hong Kong

Project Notes

Infrared Photography Hong Kong expands the official archive with a focused body of work.

This infrared project shifts Hong Kong’s garden architecture into a dreamlike register: trees become bronze and white, pavilions glow, and familiar places feel newly suspended.

The sequence is preserved as a room of its own so the rhythm between images remains legible, while the Selected Works page remains a tight exhibition edit.
