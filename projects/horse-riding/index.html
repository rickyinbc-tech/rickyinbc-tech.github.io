Photography Project / Horse Riding

Horse Riding

Equestrian motion and Hong Kong racing culture rendered as compressed colour, speed, and force.

View PhotographsAll Projects

Project Notes

Five photographs hold horse racing as a study of colour, pressure, and bodies in motion.

Riders lean into the turn, colour-coded silks cut through the frame, and the pack compresses into a single directional force.

The sequence moves between the full field and tightly framed riders. A racecourse screen briefly joins live action and public image, while rails, hooves, heads, and human posture shape each view of speed.

  • Project: Horse Riding
  • Focus: Equestrian motion and sport
  • Photographs: 5
  • First published: July 2, 2015 on Behance
