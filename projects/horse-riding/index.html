Project Notes

Horse Riding expands the official archive with a focused body of work.

A five-image project from Ricky Kwok’s Behance archive, focused on horse racing as choreography: riders leaning into the turn, colour-coded silks, and the crowded pressure of the pack.

The sequence is preserved as a room of its own so the rhythm between images remains legible, while the Selected Works page remains a tight exhibition edit.
