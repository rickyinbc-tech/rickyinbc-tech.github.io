Before Acquisition

What the written offer must confirm.

A collector can evaluate the object and the transaction before payment. No Product or Offer markup is used while these facts are not published as fixed terms.

Edition & Inventory

Edition size, artist-proof count, permitted sizes, available number, and any prior prints.

Object

Exact print process, paper or substrate, dimensions, signature, numbering, certificate, and framing specification.

Price & Production

Currency, price, payment stages, production time, proof approval where relevant, and tax treatment.

Delivery & Care

Shipping origin, eligible destination, packaging, insurance, duties, damage procedure, cancellation terms, and installation guidance.

Collector Inquiry

Request the current status of a specific work.

Include the artwork title, preferred display size, framing preference, delivery city, and whether the request is for a private, corporate, or institutional collection.
